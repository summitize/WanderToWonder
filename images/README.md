# Travel Photo Storage

## Folder Structure

- **australia/** - 30 photos from Australia trip
- **srilanka/** - 30 photos from Sri Lanka trip  
- **dubai/** - 30 photos from Dubai trip

## Image Guidelines

- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** Optimized to 200-500KB per photo
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080 or 1600x1200 recommended
- **Naming:** Use descriptive names (e.g., `sydney-opera-house.jpg`, `great-barrier-reef-01.jpg`)

## Optimization Tools

- [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/) - Online compression
- [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) - Advanced web-based optimizer
- ImageOptim (Mac) or FileOptimizer (Windows)

Upload your optimized photos to the respective destination folders.
